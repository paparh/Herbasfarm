Our Story

At herbas Farm, we produce premuim fruits and vegetables. Ensuring consistent supply of the best quality products, building strong and long-term relationships with clients.

fruits

For the production of quality planting material established root stock and bud wood banks of elite commercial varieties. Standardized drip irrigation, fertigation, rainwater harvesting and moisture conservation for higher fruit set and yield.

vegetables

The concept of Integrated Plant Nutrition Systems was introduced. The concept aims at maintaining or adjusting of soil fertility and plant nutrient supply to sustain a desired level of crop production.
