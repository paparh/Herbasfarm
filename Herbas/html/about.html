Our Story

Herbas Farm is one of Nigeria's leading digital agricultural platform. We manage approximately 37 acres of land situated in Vom the south of Jos, Plateau state, Nigeria. Where a few dozen type of exotic fruits and vegetables are grown, all grown organically with no preservatives added. We take pride in providing agricultural services that fulfill the requests of our clients. We currently farm for clients both sustainably and organically. With a short chain of command, we have the ability to execute cultural practices in a timely manner. We love our produce and take great pride in our farms. Each and every person on the farm understands their expertise and the importance of their individual role. It’s more than just planting a seed and watching it grow; there’s a whole team of people responsible for bringing it all together. From the people who prepare the land, to those who harvest the crops … all the way to the driver that delivers to your door, we all work together to bring you the very best our farms have to offer.

Our Mission

Here, our goal is for everyone to be healthy conveniently. Encouraging cash-driven market sysyem, wide market coverage, digital presence, extensive network of retailers and service providers. Delivering to you straight from the farm to your door step within 24 hours of ordering. We practice efficient production, packing, handling, storing and processing fruits and vegetables. From seed to doorstep, the produce you receive is the best local selection of fruits and vegetables grown in the most economically and environmentally sustainable way. With your support, we can help grow local farms and invest back in the local community. We believe in a transparent food system where customers can make informed choices about the food they buy and eat. Each day, we are humbled to see our company bring together our customers with other local organic farms. We are part of a food revolution, sharing and building the knowledge of a local, organic food system.

Commitment To Food Safety

Herbas Farm is committed to supplying safe, high quality fresh fruits and vegetables to its customers. We fully accept our responsibility to meet or exceed our customers’ expectations, as well as all requirements of relevant food legislation and regulations. Our produce is handled during harvesting, packing, storage, delivery and distribution in such a manner as to ensure its safety to our consumers. Our personnel are expected to observe Food Safety as a priority, and are required to abide by the Food Safety Programs that are in effect. We are committed to making the necessary resources available to support the development, implementation, and maintenance of our Food Safety Program. These resources include appropriate personnel, training, and other support elements to ensure the success of our Program. We further recognize that continuous improvement of our performance is a key objective.

Sustainability

At Herbas Farm we believe that taking care of our farms is the right thing for our future. We work very hard to farm in an environmentally friendly manner, making changes and adopting new practices as we find better ways to preserve and protect our land and conserve our resources. Our areas of focus include soil conservation practices, reducing carbon emissions, utilizing integrated pest management, energy and water conservation programs, seeking environmentally friendly packaging opportunities, and equipping our warehouses and offices with recycling programs and good energy practice reminders. We also provide our employees with ongoing training in current farming practices, safety, and a variety of out-reach programs. All of our efforts are driven by the determination to provide the highest quality of service, at reasonable rates, to allow our clients enjoy the organic farm produces we produce.
